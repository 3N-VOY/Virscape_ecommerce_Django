Shipping and Returns

Shipping Information

We offer standard shipping and expedited shipping options. Shipping times may vary depending on your location and the items you order. You will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information once your order has been shipped.

Return Policy

If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return it within 30 days of receiving your order. Please contact our customer support team to initiate the return process. Returned items must be in their original condition and packaging.

Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will process your refund within 5-7 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our shipping and returns policy, please contact us at support@example.com.
